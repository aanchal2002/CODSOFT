A Bakery is a cooking establishment where a wide variety of baked goods, such as breads, pasteries, cakes, and cookies are preapared and sold, often offering a delightful way to satisfy one's sweet and savory cravings. Lucky for us we live near by Kolkata, is renowned for its rich and diverse bakery culture by producing delicious baked goods, including unique items like "SANDESH" and "ROSOGOLLA" for which the state is very famous across globe.
